Help Center

How do I place an order?

Browse products on the Shop page, click “Add to Cart”, and proceed to checkout. This website is a demo project and does not process real payments.

Can I modify my cart?

Yes, you can add or remove items from your cart before checkout. Cart data is stored locally in your browser.

Do you offer customer support?

For any queries, you can reach us via the Contact page. This project is created for educational purposes.

Are the products real?

Product images and descriptions are for demonstration only and do not represent real commercial sales.

Is my data secure?

No sensitive data or payment information is collected on this website.
